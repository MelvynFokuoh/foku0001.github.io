About Me

My name is Melvyn Fokuoh. I'm a screenwriter and an aspiring web developer. I joined this program initially for design, but I have already pivoted towards programming. It has so far been my favourite course in the IMD program and I'm hoping to learn more and advance my skills. I'm aiming to do some self-learning on the side as well.

At first I was wondering why I was instantly drawn to programming in week one, then I realized it was one of the same reasons I love screenwriting: the creation of a blueprint. As a screenwriter, many people are surprised that I don't like any other type of writing other than screenwriting and songwriting. These forms of writing are essentially a blueprint of text before they are consumed by others in a visual and/or sonic form.
